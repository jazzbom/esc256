We Are

Our proprietary blockchain leveraged solutions are helping private Island Owners, Real Estate Brokers and Art Dealers to create, manage and safely sell NFT's that are truly backed by tangible asset value.

What We Do

It's not just digital art that can be NFT'ed! Our upgraded NFT protocol mitigates risks of fraud & ownership right disputes through an introduction of a non-owner representative(Agent) acting as a trusted party and a central authority(CR) on behalf of the token owner.

Private Owners

Widen the reach of your private investments in art, real estate or even a private island by digitizing it into an asset-backed NFT on the Ethereum blockchain and open a new world with boundless possibilities energised by DeFi.

Art Dealers & Auctioneers

Mint, manage or sell NFT's in both digital art as well as real asset-backed items on behalf of your clients. Our expert team would help you set up a secure infrastructure.

Real-Estate Dealers

Auction off your wine glass shaped island to the digital crowd! Receive the right tools and expertise to work with property owners & buyers interested in Real estate backed NFT.
