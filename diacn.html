DIACN VERIFIEDTM

A Blockchain based solution to safeguard academic institutions, employers and students from degree fraud.

DIACN VerifiedTM system is an academic certificate verification service offering cost-effective instant certificate verification for employers and students whilst providing academic institutions with a zero cost solution to secure their printed certificates.

How does DIACN VerifiedTM work?

  1. The system generates a unique DIACN record for a certificate and stores it on to the Blockchain. The Digital Identification Academic Certificate Number, abbreviated as DIACN is a unique digital content identifier for non-digital (in paper form) certificates issued by academic organisations. A DIACN when stored on a decentralized, distributed and public digital ledger like the Ethereum Blockchain acts like a proof-of-existence of a certificate issued to an individual.
  2. The system then produces a QR code for the unique DIACN record which links to our verification service. By printing this QR code on a certificate, anyone can scan the QR code using any smartphone and access the verification service for a small fee.
  3. The verification service is built on the Ethereum Smart contracts technology. The Smart contracts which are running on the Blockchain, guarantee the authenticity of the verified results.
  4. To prevent fraud and fake duplicates, the service applies the Scan & Burn principle which forces the certificate owner to re-authorise the use of this service after each verification. In other words, a certificate can only be verified once, after which the genuine owner of the certificate must re-authorise it for the next use. This effectively stops a duplicate certificate with the same QR from being copied and used without the owner's permission.
  5. Each verified result costs the user who is scanning the QR code a small fee. This minimum charge is there to prevent multiple unauthorised and misuse of the service as after each verification result, the certificate owner is required to re-authorise its use.

Benefits of using DIACN VerifiedTM ?

  • Instant verification/authentication of any certificate.
  • Unique Scan & Burn technology to stop forgery and fakes.
  • Zero costs to the certificate issuing authority once the certificate record is added to the Blockchain through our service.
  • Cheaper and faster than any other current certificate verification service due to our advance Blockchain leveraged system.
  • Improves certificate issuing authority's brand value and image as using a Blockchain based solution shows your commitment to authenticity, honesty and willingness to embrace newer and better technologies.
  • By using a Blockchain based service, your certificate record is automatically entitled to all the advantages of Blockchain like permanent, indestructible and immutable digital record which can be accessed anytime, from anywhere, with or without us for as long as computers (in this case Ethereum nodes) exist.
